Welcome to PlanVIEW


What is PlanVIEW?
PlanVIEW is an all-in-one school app. Take quick notes and access your teacher's websites in a clean and simple user interface. Let's get started with the simple setup.

Links Setup
  1. Press the (menu) button in the top left of the app
  2. Scroll down to the tab that says Settings and press it
  3. Next, go to your browser on your phone (Chrome or Safari) and open up your First Block Teacher's Website. Copy the link from the address bar.
  4. Go back to the PlanVIEW app and in the Settings, paste the link in the box that says 1st Block Website Link
  5. Do it this for the rest of your classes.
  6. Click Save. You should get message that says Settings Saved

Important Note

If your class doesn't have a website or you do not use the website, you MUST still put a link of a website in that box. It can be important websites you visit or something like google.com. The option for Off-block or No Links will be available in our PRO Version

Now, if you ever have to go to your teacher's website quickly then you can click (menu) button and then the tab you need instead of searching for it on your browser
